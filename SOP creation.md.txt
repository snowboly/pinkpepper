Cleaning and Disinfection SOP
Document No.: PP-CLEANING-SOP-001

Version: 1.0

Date: 16/03/2026

Approved By: Kitchen Manager

Scope: This SOP applies to all staff members working in the kitchen premises, including morning staff and line managers, and outlines the procedures for cleaning and disinfecting all surfaces, equipment, walls, and floors.

Purpose
The purpose of this SOP is to ensure that the kitchen premises are cleaned and disinfected to a high standard, in compliance with current EU/UK food safety legislation, to prevent contamination and ensure a safe environment for food preparation.

Objectives
The objectives of this SOP are to: ensure all surfaces, equipment, walls, and floors are cleaned and disinfected regularly; prevent the growth of microorganisms; and maintain a clean and hygienic environment.

Responsibilities
The morning staff and line managers are responsible for ensuring that the cleaning and disinfection procedures are carried out as outlined in this SOP. The line manager is responsible for overseeing the cleaning schedule and ensuring that all staff members are trained in the procedures.

Training
All staff members must receive training on the cleaning and disinfection procedures before commencing work in the kitchen. The training will be provided by the line manager and will include a review of this SOP.

Materials/Chemicals
The following cleaning chemicals will be used: Diversey products. These products have been selected for their effectiveness and compliance with EU/UK food safety legislation.

Storage and Handling
All cleaning chemicals will be stored in a designated area, away from food and food preparation areas. Staff members must handle the chemicals with care, wearing protective gloves and eyewear as necessary.

Step-by-Step Procedure
The following steps will be taken to clean and disinfect the kitchen premises: 1) Remove any food debris and waste; 2) Sweep and mop the floors; 3) Clean and disinfect all surfaces, equipment, and walls using Diversey products; 4) Sanitize all utensils and equipment.

Specific Cleaning Tasks
The following specific cleaning tasks will be carried out: daily cleaning of the kitchen counters and sinks; weekly cleaning of the kitchen floors and walls; and monthly deep cleaning of the kitchen equipment and utensils.

Frequency Schedule
The cleaning and disinfection procedures will be carried out at the following frequencies: daily, weekly, and monthly. The line manager will review and update the cleaning schedule as necessary.

Cleaning Schedule
The cleaning schedule will be posted in the kitchen and will include the following tasks and frequencies: daily cleaning of the kitchen counters and sinks; weekly cleaning of the kitchen floors and walls; and monthly deep cleaning of the kitchen equipment and utensils.

Verification
The line manager will verify that the cleaning and disinfection procedures are being carried out as outlined in this SOP. Verification will include: visual inspections of the kitchen premises; review of the cleaning schedule; and monitoring of staff member training and compliance.

Corrective Action
In the event of non-compliance with this SOP, the line manager will take corrective action, including: re-training staff members; re-cleaning and disinfecting the affected area; and reviewing and updating the cleaning schedule as necessary.

Records
The following records will be kept: cleaning schedule; staff member training records; and verification records. These records will be reviewed and updated regularly by the line manager.

Sign-off Log
A sign-off log will be maintained to record the completion of cleaning and disinfection tasks. The log will include the date, task, and signature of the staff member responsible for completing the task.
