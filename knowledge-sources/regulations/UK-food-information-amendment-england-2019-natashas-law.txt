The Food Information (Amendment) (England) Regulations 2019 introduced the England-level change commonly known as Natasha's Law.

Why it matters:
- Strengthens allergen labelling requirements for prepacked for direct sale (PPDS) food
- Important for classification of PPDS vs non-PPDS food
- Relevant to labels, recipe control, and allergen-change management

Use this source when the question concerns:
- Natasha's Law
- PPDS food
- allergen labels for food packed on site for direct sale
- whether a package needs named allergen and ingredient information
