The Official Feed and Food Controls (England) Regulations 2009 are part of the domestic enforcement framework for official controls in England.

Why it matters:
- Adds England-level official-control enforcement detail
- Relevant to officer powers, appointments, notices, and penalties
- Complements EU official-controls law with domestic enforcement mechanics
