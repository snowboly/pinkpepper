COMMISSION REGULATION (EU) 2021/382
of 3 March 2021
amending the Annexes to Regulation (EC) No 852/2004 on the hygiene of foodstuffs as regards food allergen management, redistribution of food and food safety culture

OFFICIAL SOURCE
- EUR-Lex ELI: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2021/382/oj/eng
- OJ L 74, 4.3.2021, pp. 3-6
- CELEX: 32021R0382

LEGAL PURPOSE
- Updates the hygiene framework in Regulation (EC) No 852/2004
- Brings food allergen management, redistribution of surplus food, and food safety culture more clearly into the hygiene baseline

KEY LEGAL EFFECTS

1. FOOD ALLERGEN MANAGEMENT
- Allergen control is not only a labelling issue under Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011
- Hygiene controls must also address the risk of cross-contact from allergenic substances
- Equipment, containers, transport, storage, and handling practices need to be considered where allergens are present

2. REDISTRIBUTION OF FOOD
- Surplus food may be redistributed for human consumption where it is safe to do so
- Food business operators remain responsible for assessing safety and suitability
- Key practical factors include:
  - shelf life and date marking
  - storage temperatures
  - packaging integrity
  - traceability

3. FOOD SAFETY CULTURE
- Food safety culture is incorporated into the hygiene framework
- Management should support:
  - leadership and commitment
  - staff awareness
  - open communication on food safety issues
  - training and supervision
  - sufficient resources and clear responsibilities

WHY THIS MATTERS FOR RETRIEVAL
- Useful for answers about whether food safety culture is a legal expectation
- Useful for allergen-management questions focused on operations rather than label wording
- Useful for questions about food donations and redistribution under hygiene law

USE WITH
