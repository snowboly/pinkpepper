The Food Safety and Hygiene (England) Regulations 2013 are a main England-level domestic enforcement instrument for food hygiene and safety.

Why it matters:
- Provides offences, powers, notices, penalties, and procedural enforcement detail
- Important for inspection and local-authority enforcement reasoning
- Sits underneath practical enforcement of hygiene rules in England

Use this source when the question concerns:
- England-specific hygiene enforcement
- officer powers
- notices, detention, prohibition, or prosecution
- domestic enforcement detail for hygiene non-compliance
