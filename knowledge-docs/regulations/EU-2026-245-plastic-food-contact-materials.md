# Commission Regulation (EU) 2026/245 - Plastic Food Contact Materials

## Overview
Commission Regulation (EU) 2026/245 of 2 February 2026 amends Annex I to Regulation (EU) No 10/2011 on plastic materials and articles intended to come into contact with food.
