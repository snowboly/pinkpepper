# Commission Regulation (EU) 2026/250 - Bisphenol Food Contact Corrections

## Overview
Commission Regulation (EU) 2026/250 corrects Regulation (EU) 2024/3190 on bisphenols and hazardous bisphenol derivatives in food contact materials and articles.

- Jurisdiction: EU
- Source type: regulation
- Published: February 2026
- CELEX: 32026R0250
- Official source: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2026/250/oj

## Why it matters
- It corrects transitional provisions and related wording in the new bisphenol food contact regime.
- It matters for businesses using coatings, varnishes, metal packaging, or other food contact articles affected by BPA restrictions.

## Practical retrieval points
- Use this source for questions on transition dates and market placement for legacy BPA-affected food contact articles.
- The correction is important because transitional wording and dates affect what may still be first placed on the market and for how long certain articles may remain on the market.

## Compliance implications
- Businesses should not rely on the uncorrected wording of Regulation (EU) 2024/3190.
- Transition planning for single-use and repeat-use food contact articles should use the corrected dates and categories.
- Packaging and procurement teams should recheck any BPA-related stock depletion plans against the corrected timetable.
