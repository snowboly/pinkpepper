# Commission Regulation (EU) 2025/351 - Plastic Food Contact Materials

## Overview
Commission Regulation (EU) 2025/351 updates Regulation (EU) No 10/2011 on plastic materials and articles intended to come into contact with food.

- Jurisdiction: EU
- Source type: regulation
- Published: 24 February 2025
- CELEX: 32025R0351
- Official source: https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX:32025R0351

## Why it matters
- It updates the Union list and related compositional rules for plastic food contact materials.
- It is relevant when assessing declarations of compliance, migration limits, and permitted substance use in packaging or processing-contact plastics.

## Practical retrieval points
- Regulation (EU) No 10/2011 remains the core EU plastics measure, but this amendment changes what is permitted and under what conditions.
- Businesses using plastic packaging, plastic process equipment components, or plastic multilayer materials should verify that supplier declarations reflect the amended authorisations and restrictions.
- Food businesses should expect questions on:
  - whether a specific substance is still authorised
  - whether new restrictions apply to a substance already in use
  - whether declarations of compliance and supporting evidence need updating

## Compliance implications
- Check current supplier declarations of compliance against the amended Union list.
- Review any internal packaging specifications that still rely on pre-2025 assumptions.
- Reconfirm migration-testing assumptions where a substance has new conditions or limits.
