# Official Feed and Food Controls (England) Regulations 2009

## Overview
The Official Feed and Food Controls (England) Regulations 2009 form part of the domestic enforcement framework for official controls in England. They are relevant to how authorities exercise powers, appoint officers, and take enforcement action in relation to food and feed controls.

- **FSA guidance hub:** https://www.food.gov.uk/business-guidance/general-food-law
- **Legislation.gov.uk source:** https://www.legislation.gov.uk/uksi/2009/3255/contents/made

## Core Practical Themes

### 1. Domestic Official-Control Machinery
The regulation supports how official controls are carried out and enforced within England.

### 2. Competent Authority Powers
It helps define who can act, under what authority, and through what procedures.

### 3. Notices, Action, and Penalties
The regulation supports enforcement routes where operators fail to comply with applicable food-control obligations.
