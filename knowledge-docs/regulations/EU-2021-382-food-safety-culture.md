# Commission Regulation (EU) 2021/382 — Food Safety Culture, Food Redistribution, and Allergen Management

## Overview
Commission Regulation (EU) 2021/382 of 3 March 2021 amends Annexes to Regulation (EC) No 852/2004. It is important because it updates the hygiene framework to reflect newer expectations around allergen control, safe redistribution of food, and food safety culture.

- **CELEX:** 32021R0382
- **Amends:** Regulation (EC) No 852/2004
- **Official source:** https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2021/382/oj/eng
- **OJ:** L 74, 4.3.2021, pp. 3-6

---

## Why It Matters
This regulation turns three topics that were often treated as guidance-only into clearer legal expectations within the hygiene framework:

1. **Food allergen management**
2. **Redistribution of surplus food**
3. **Food safety culture**

For PinkPepper, that means these topics should be treated as part of the legal hygiene baseline, not only as best practice.

---

## Main Changes

### 1. Food Allergen Management
The amendment updates the hygiene annexes so allergen control is treated as part of good hygiene practice, not only as a labelling issue under Regulation (EU) No 1169/2011.

Practical implications:
- prevent or reduce cross-contact from allergenic substances during harvesting, transport, storage, preparation, and processing
- review cleaning, changeover, storage, and utensil controls where allergen and non-allergen foods are handled
- treat allergen contamination risk as part of operational hygiene design

This is especially relevant when answering questions about:
- allergen cleaning validation
- segregation of allergenic ingredients
- utensil and container controls
- how allergen management fits into HACCP and prerequisite programmes

### 2. Redistribution of Food
The amendment adds explicit recognition that surplus food may be redistributed where it is safe to do so.

Practical implications:
- businesses redistributing food must assess whether food remains safe and suitable
- decisions should consider shelf life, storage conditions, packaging integrity, and traceability
- redistribution does not remove the operator's hygiene responsibilities

This matters for:
- food donations
- charity redistribution
- use-by vs best-before decisions
- handling of chilled surplus foods

### 3. Food Safety Culture
The amendment incorporates food safety culture into the legal hygiene framework.

In practical terms, management should support:
- commitment to safe food handling
- clear communication of food safety expectations
- staff awareness of hazards and controls
- training and supervision
- allocation of sufficient resources and responsibilities

This does not replace HACCP. It strengthens the expectation that businesses create the conditions for HACCP and hygiene controls to work in practice.

---

## Interaction with Existing Law

- **EC 852/2004:** 2021/382 amends the hygiene annexes of the core hygiene regulation
- **EU 1169/2011:** allergen information law still applies, but 2021/382 reinforces the hygiene-management side of allergen control
- **Codex CXC 1-1969:** the regulation reflects the updated Codex General Principles of Food Hygiene, including food safety culture
