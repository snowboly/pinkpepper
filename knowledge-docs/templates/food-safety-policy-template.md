## 1. Policy Statement

_[Business Name]_ is committed to producing and serving food that is safe, legal, and of the quality expected by our customers. This policy provides the framework for our food safety management system and underpins all operational procedures.

We will:
- Comply with all applicable food safety legislation, including **Regulation (EC) 852/2004**, the **Food Safety Act 1990**, the **Food Safety and Hygiene (England) Regulations 2013** (or devolved equivalents), and any applicable EU retained law
- Operate a documented food safety management system based on HACCP principles (Codex Alimentarius)
- Provide adequate resources, training, and supervision to all staff
- Continuously review and improve our food safety performance

## 6. HACCP Commitment

The business operates a documented HACCP plan (or HACCP-based food safety management system) that:
- Identifies all biological, chemical, physical, and allergen hazards
- Determines critical control points (CCPs) and critical limits
- Establishes monitoring, corrective action, and verification procedures
- Is reviewed annually and upon any significant change

Supporting prerequisite programmes (PRPs) are maintained for: supplier approval, cleaning, pest control, personal hygiene, training, traceability, maintenance, water supply, and waste management.
