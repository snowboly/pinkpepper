This procedure establishes the traceability system for _[Business Name]_, ensuring all food products and ingredients can be traced one step back (to the supplier) and one step forward (to the customer/recipient) as required by **Regulation (EC) 178/2002, Articles 17–20** (General Food Law).

## 7. Recall and Withdrawal Procedure

### 7.1 Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Withdrawal** | Removal of product from the supply chain (not yet reached the consumer) |
| **Recall** | Removal of product from the consumer (public notification required) |

### 7.2 Decision Tree

```
Product identified as potentially unsafe
        ↓
Has the product reached the final consumer?
    NO → WITHDRAWAL
        - Notify customers to stop use/sale
        - Retrieve affected stock
        - Quarantine and investigate
    YES → RECALL
        - Notify Local Authority / FSA immediately
        - Issue public recall notice
        - Notify customers to return product
        - Quarantine remaining stock
        ↓
Investigate root cause
        ↓
Implement corrective action
        ↓
Document all actions taken
```
