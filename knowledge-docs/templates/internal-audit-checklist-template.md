## Audit Scoring

**Conformance Levels:**
- **C (Conforming):** Requirement fully met
- **NC (Non-Conforming):** Requirement not met; corrective action required
  - **Critical NC:** Immediate food safety risk
  - **Major NC:** Significant system failure
  - **Minor NC:** Isolated incident or low-risk issue
- **N/A:** Not applicable
