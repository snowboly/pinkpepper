## 1. Purpose

This procedure defines the pest prevention, monitoring, and control measures for _[Business Name]_ to protect food safety and comply with **Regulation (EC) 852/2004, Annex II, Chapter I (2)(c)** and **Chapter IX (4)**, which require adequate procedures to control pests.

## 3. Types of Pests Covered

| Pest Category | Examples | Key Risks |
|---|---|---|
| **Rodents** | Rats (Rattus norvegicus), mice (Mus musculus) | Contamination (droppings, urine, hair); structural damage; disease transmission (Salmonella, Leptospirosis) |
| **Crawling insects** | Cockroaches (Blattella germanica, Periplaneta americana), ants | Contamination; pathogen transfer; allergen risk |
| **Flying insects** | Houseflies, fruit flies, drain flies | Physical contamination; pathogen transfer |
| **Stored product insects (SPI)** | Flour beetles, Indian meal moth, weevils | Product contamination; economic loss |
| **Birds** | Pigeons, starlings, gulls | Droppings; feathers; parasites; disease (Psittacosis) |

## 4. Prevention Measures

### 4.1 Proofing

| Area | Measure |
|---|---|
| External doors | Self-closing; no gaps >6mm at base; brush strips or rubber seals |
| Windows | Fly screens fitted to openable windows in food areas |
| Pipe / cable entry points | Sealed with cement, steel wool, or expanding foam |
| Roof and eaves | No gaps or broken tiles; bird netting/spikes where needed |
| Drainage | Drain covers intact; gullies free of debris |
| Loading bays | Strip curtains or rapid-close doors fitted |

### 4.2 Housekeeping

- No food left uncovered or on floors overnight
- Waste bins emptied daily; external bins with tight-fitting lids
- Spillages cleaned immediately
- Stored goods raised off floor on racking (minimum 150mm clearance)
- External areas kept clear of vegetation, litter, and standing water within 1m of building

## 8. Corrective Actions

| Trigger | Action |
|---|---|
| Pest sighting (single instance) | Record in sighting log; inspect area; deep clean; monitor; notify contractor if needed |
| Pest activity detected by contractor | Review contractor report; implement recommendations; increase monitoring frequency |
| Evidence of infestation | Quarantine affected products; contact contractor for emergency treatment; investigate source; deep clean; review proofing measures |
| Product contamination by pests | Quarantine and dispose of affected product; investigate; document corrective action; notify EHO if required |
