## Notes for Suppliers

1. **Intentionally added allergens:** Allergens used as ingredients in the product formulation.
2. **Cross-contamination risk:** Allergens not in the product but present in the same facility, on shared equipment, or in raw materials from suppliers.
3. **"May contain" statements:** Use only where genuine cross-contamination risk exists and cannot be eliminated through cleaning or segregation. Avoid blanket "may contain" statements not supported by risk assessment.
4. **Notification of changes:** You must inform the customer **in advance** of any changes to ingredients, suppliers, or manufacturing processes that could affect allergen status.
5. **Traceability:** Ensure full traceability of allergen-containing ingredients (supplier batch codes, delivery records).
