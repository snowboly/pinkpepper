# Phase 1 Organic Traffic Plan

## Objective

Build a credible SEO foundation for PinkPepper so the site can rank for high-intent food safety compliance queries and convert that traffic into signups and sales conversations.

## Current audit

### Strengths

- Clear niche positioning: AI food safety compliance for EU and UK food businesses.
- Good core commercial pages already exist: home, pricing, about, security, contact.
- Strong topical authority signals in copy: HACCP, allergens, SOPs, audit prep, EUR-Lex.
- Canonicals and robots are already implemented across much of the app.

### Gaps

- Search coverage is too thin. There are no dedicated feature pages, use-case pages, industry pages, or comparison pages.
- The homepage tries to rank for too many intents at once instead of owning one primary category term.
- Auth pages were indexable or listed in the sitemap despite having low SEO value.
- Social preview metadata referenced `/og-image.png`, but no such asset existed.
- Several indexed pages contained mojibake in visible copy and metadata, which degrades trust and CTR.
- There is no blog or resource center yet, so topical authority cannot compound.

## Positioning recommendation

Use one primary category narrative consistently across the homepage and shared metadata:

`AI food safety compliance software for EU and UK food businesses`

Supporting terms should reinforce, not replace, that category:

- HACCP plan generation
- allergen documentation
- SOP generation
- audit preparation
- food safety records

## Internal linking rules

- Every public page should link back to `/pricing` and at least one future feature or use-case page.
- Homepage sections should route users into dedicated money pages instead of keeping all intent on `/`.
- Footer navigation should eventually include `Features`, `Use Cases`, and `Resources`.
- Comparison and editorial pages should push users to feature and pricing pages with descriptive anchors.

## Measurement plan

Track weekly in Search Console and analytics:

- non-brand clicks
- impressions by landing page
- CTR for homepage and pricing
- indexed page count
- top queries containing `food safety`, `HACCP`, `allergen`, and `audit`
- signups from organic landing pages
- contact submissions from organic landing pages

## Phase 1 completion criteria

- Core public pages are clean, indexable, and free of broken metadata.
- Auth pages are noindexed and removed from the sitemap.
- Social sharing image is valid.
- One category message is used consistently across the public site.
- A 10-page SEO build backlog is ready for Phase 2.
