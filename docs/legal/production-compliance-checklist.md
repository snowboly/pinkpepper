## EU AI Act launch notes

Before production launch, document whether PinkPepper is acting as an AI-system provider, deployer, distributor, or only a downstream service using third-party GPAI models, and keep evidence for the applicable AI Act duties. AI literacy obligations applied from 2 February 2025. General-purpose AI model provider obligations applied from 2 August 2025. Article 50 transparency obligations apply from 2 August 2026, including informing users when they interact with an AI system and labelling covered AI-generated/manipulated content where applicable.

References for launch review: European Commission AI Act implementation timeline and the Commission guidance/code materials for GPAI and Article 50 transparency.
