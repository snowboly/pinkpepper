# Pricing Page Rewrite Design

**Goal:** Rewrite the pricing page so it reflects the current product truth in a tighter, more human commercial style.

**Direction:**
- Replace outdated entitlement language.
- Remove hypey framing and stale AI-heavy copy.
- Reduce repetition and make the page easier to trust.

**Structure:**
- Simple hero and subhead
- Clean tier grid for Free, Plus, and Pro
- One concise comparison/entitlement section
- Short FAQ
- Final contact CTA

**Copy constraints:**
- Use plain, human language.
- Do not promise PDF export for standard users.
- Keep consultancy as a real Pro differentiator, not a vague marketing flourish.
- Sell the plans by what operators can actually do.
