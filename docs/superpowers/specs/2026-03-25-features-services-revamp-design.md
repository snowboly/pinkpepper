# Features Page Services Revamp Design

**Goal:** Rework the `/features` page so it reads like a concise services page written by a person, while still selling PinkPepper's core commercial capabilities.

**Direction:**
- Keep the `/features` route for now.
- Replace feature-hub language with service-led commercial language.
- Avoid overt AI/SaaS phrasing.
- Use service cards to sell concrete capabilities and outputs.

**Structure:**
- Hero with plain-language service framing and `Talk to us` as the primary CTA.
- Expanded set of service cards covering core commercial capabilities.
- Each card includes a short description, compact included-items list, and deeper link where relevant.
- Short supporting sections for what is included and why teams use PinkPepper.
- CTA footer with `Talk to us` and `See pricing`.

**Card set:**
- HACCP plans
- Allergen records
- SOPs and hygiene procedures
- Audit prep and corrective actions
- Downloadable compliance templates
- Conversation export and handover
- Specialist review support
- Virtual audit workflows

**Constraints:**
- Keep the page concise.
- Preserve useful SEO behavior and internal linking.
- Write in a human, practical voice.
