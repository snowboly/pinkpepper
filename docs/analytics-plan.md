# Analytics Operating Plan

## Objective

Track whether PinkPepper's new SEO page clusters are earning impressions, clicks, and conversions, then use that data to decide what to expand, rewrite, or prune.

## Page groups

- homepage: `/`
- pricing and trust: `/pricing`, `/about`, `/security`, `/contact`
- features: `/features/*`
- use cases: `/use-cases/*`
- comparisons: `/compare/*`
- resources: `/resources/*`

## Weekly checks

Review these in Google Search Console and product analytics once per week:

- non-brand clicks
- impressions by page group
- top queries by page
- average position for target pages
- CTR for homepage, pricing, and top landing pages
- signups by landing page
- contact submissions by landing page

## Questions to answer

### Visibility

- Which new pages are getting impressions but few clicks?
- Which pages are not indexed or not receiving impressions?
- Which query themes are emerging that deserve new pages?

### Conversion

- Which page groups drive the highest signup rate?
- Do resource pages assist signups even if they do not convert directly?
- Which pages need stronger CTA placement or internal links?

### Content quality

- Which pages rank but sit below page-one thresholds?
- Which pages need more depth, examples, or comparison detail?
- Which pages are too thin and should be merged rather than expanded?

## Monthly actions

- refresh titles and descriptions on low-CTR pages
- expand pages that get impressions but weak ranking
- add internal links from new resources into winning feature pages
- prune or combine pages that show no search traction after a reasonable indexation period

## Success signals

- more indexed pages across `features`, `use-cases`, `compare`, and `resources`
- rising non-brand impressions
- first clicks on comparison and use-case pages
- resource pages beginning to assist signups
- pricing and feature pages receiving internal-link support from resource content
